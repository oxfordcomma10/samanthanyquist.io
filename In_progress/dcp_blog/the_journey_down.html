From Minnesota to Florida
On a glorious Wednesday afternoon my dad, mom, and I began our journey down to the "Sunshine State" from the Twin Cities. Packed to the brim, my poor 2008 Mazda CX-7 contained my entire closet within vacuum sealed bags, boxes of decorations, and lots of pillows.

We started at home and drove South down through Iowa with a stop in a Cedar Rapids Chic-fil-A for a quick dinner and bathroom break. I mostly slept/rested during this portion of the roadtrip. After dinner we continued on through the rest of Iowa and onto Missouri with a quck gas fill up somewhere in there. In Missouri I remembered that I had my laptop accessible and wanted to get this portion of my website started, since coding takes so long and doesn't require internet access, the long car hours seemed like the perfect time to code.
